How It Works

From Symptoms to Smart Diagnosis

A simple 4-step process powered by machine learning to deliver rapid health assessments and intelligent triage.

01

Input Health Data

Patients enter their symptoms, vital parameters (BP, heart rate, temperature, SpO2), and basic health information through a simple form.

02

AI Analysis

Our Random Forest ML model processes the data, analyzing patterns across 25+ health indicators to predict risk levels.

03

Triage Classification

The system categorizes urgency into Emergency, Urgent, or Routine, with confidence scores and contributing factor analysis.

04

Hospital Resource Allocation

Healthcare providers receive prioritized patient lists, enabling efficient resource allocation and timely medical interventions.
